About Us

Captain Kal’s is a seafood market and steamer in Bartlett, Tennessee focused on fresh seafood, steamed-to-order platters, lobster favorites, signature sides, and coastal comfort food.

Whether you are stopping in for dinner, grabbing takeout, or looking for a seafood spread to share, Captain Kal’s brings fresh flavor and a casual seafood-market atmosphere.
